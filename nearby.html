📍 Nearby Places & Day Trips

Short drives. Big payoff. These are the easiest "let's go do something" options near Surfside.

🌊 Murrells Inlet

Historic fishing village and the Seafood Capital of South Carolina. MarshWalk sunsets, waterfront dining, and live music.

🌾 Pawleys Island

Lowcountry charm with quieter beaches and a slower pace. Home of the iconic rope hammocks and classic coastal scenery.

🎡 Myrtle Beach

Boardwalk energy, shopping, shows, mini golf, and nightlife — easy to visit without staying in the crowds.

🏙️ Conway

A charming historic downtown with riverfront views, local shops, and restaurants along the Waccamaw River.

⚓ Georgetown Waterfront

One of South Carolina's oldest cities — beautiful waterfront, historic district, and great seafood spots.

🌿 Brookgreen Gardens

America's first public sculpture garden — stunning outdoor art, wildlife preserve, and Lowcountry nature trails.

🦐 Calabash

The Seafood Capital of the World — a short drive to North Carolina with legendary all-you-can-eat seafood restaurants.

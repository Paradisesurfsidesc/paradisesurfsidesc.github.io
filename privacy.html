Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Who We Are

Paradise is a vacation rental property located in Surfside Beach, South Carolina, operated by Paradise LLC. Our website is paradisesurfsidesc.com.

What Data We Collect

When you sign up for our mailing list, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • ZIP code
  • How you heard about Paradise
  • Approximate travel dates
  • Date and time of signup

Why We Collect It

We collect this information to send you occasional emails about promotions, availability, and local tips related to Paradise vacation rental. We will never sell or share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

How We Store It

Your data is stored securely by Klaviyo, our email marketing platform. Klaviyo is GDPR compliant and maintains appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. You can read Klaviyo's privacy policy at klaviyo.com/legal/privacy-notice.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time and your data will be removed from our active mailing list. To request full deletion of your data, contact us at the email below.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to request deletion of your data, please contact us at:

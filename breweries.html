🍺 Local Breweries

The Myrtle Beach Beer Trail runs right through the area — great for post-beach afternoons, rainy days, and anyone who likes a well-crafted pint.

🗺️ Myrtle Beach Beer Trail

The official map and guide to every craft brewery on the trail — plan your route, track your passport stamps, and discover what's new.

🏭 New South Brewing

One of South Carolina's original craft breweries, open since 1995. A Myrtle Beach institution — core lagers, IPAs, and seasonals brewed on-site.

~ 10 min from Paradise

🔨 Crooked Hammock Brewery

A beach-town brewery with a massive outdoor space, fire pits, lawn games, and rotating taps. Great food, kid-friendly atmosphere, and a true hang.

~ 15 min from Paradise

🐶 Dead Dog Saloon

A Murrells Inlet staple on the Marshwalk — live music, cold drinks, and a waterfront porch that's hard to leave. More bar than brewery but a must-visit.

~ 20 min from Paradise

⚓ River City Brewing

Conway's craft brewery, tucked into a historic building by the Waccamaw River. Rotating taps, good food, and a laid-back local crowd.

~ 30 min — worth the drive

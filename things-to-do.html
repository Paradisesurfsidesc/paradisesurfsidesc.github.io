📍 Nearby Places & Day Trips

Short drives. Big payoff. These are the easiest "let's go do something" options near Surfside — great for 3-day stays and still worth repeating during a full week.

🏖️ Surfside Beach

Known as "The Family Beach" — relaxed, uncrowded, and easy. Hit the sand, walk the pier area, and keep it simple.

⚡ Great for 3-Day Stays

🌊 Murrells Inlet

Historic fishing village and the Seafood Capital of South Carolina. MarshWalk sunsets, waterfront dining, and live music.

⚡ Great for 3-Day Stays 🗓️ Perfect for Week Stays

🌾 Pawleys Island

Lowcountry charm with quieter beaches and a slower pace. Home of the iconic rope hammocks and classic coastal scenery.

🗓️ Best if You're Here All Week

🎡 Myrtle Beach

Boardwalk energy, shopping, shows, mini golf, and nightlife — easy to visit without staying in the crowds.

⚡ Great for 3-Day Stays 🗓️ Perfect for Week Stays

🎢 Attractions

The best things to do in and around Surfside Beach — from state parks and beaches to mini golf, aquariums, and day trips.

Right Here

🏖️ Surfside Beach

The quiet, family-friendly beach right out front — less crowded than Myrtle Beach with free public beach access and a relaxed vibe. No drive needed.

🎣 Garden City Pier

One of the longest fishing piers on the East Coast. Walk out over the Atlantic, fish, or just enjoy the views — great at sunrise.

🏖️ Myrtle Beach State Park

A stunning stretch of natural beach with hiking trails through maritime forest. One of the best spots on the Grand Strand for nature and a quieter swim. ~ 5 min.

Myrtle Beach

🎭 Broadway at the Beach

The Grand Strand's biggest shopping, dining, and entertainment complex — mini golf, restaurants, live entertainment, and a movie theater on a lake.

🦈 Ripley's Aquarium

Walk through a tunnel with sharks overhead — kid-friendly, beautiful exhibits, and consistently one of the area's top-rated attractions.

🎡 Family Kingdom Amusement Park

A classic beachfront amusement park with a wooden roller coaster, rides for all ages, and an old-school summer feel.

🌊 Myrtle Beach Boardwalk

A 1.2-mile oceanfront promenade lined with restaurants, shops, and street performers. Great for an evening stroll or a weekend morning walk.

🔬 WonderWorks

An upside-down building full of interactive science and adventure exhibits — a solid rainy-day option for kids and curious adults.

🌊 Myrtle Waves Water Park

The largest water park in South Carolina — wave pool, lazy river, thrill slides, and a kids' splash zone.

⛳ Hawaiian Rumble Mini Golf

One of the most scenic mini golf courses on the Strand — lush tropical landscaping, waterfalls, and a live volcano.

🐊 Alligator Adventure

Over 800 reptiles including rare albino alligators and enormous Nile crocs. Located inside Barefoot Landing — surprisingly impressive.

🎸 Barefoot Landing

An open-air waterfront shopping and entertainment district with great restaurants, live music stages, and evening atmosphere.

Day Trips Worth Making

🦩 Huntington Beach State Park

One of the best-preserved beaches on the Atlantic coast — exceptional bird watching, a historic castle (Atalaya), and pristine marshes. ~ 30 min south.

🌸 Brookgreen Gardens

America's first public sculpture garden, set among ancient live oaks draped in Spanish moss. Stunning year-round. Right next to Huntington. ~ 30 min.

🦞 Murrells Inlet Marshwalk

The Seafood Capital of South Carolina — a mile-long boardwalk lined with restaurants, bars, and waterfront views. Perfect for a long dinner out. ~ 20 min.

⛵ Georgetown Historic District

South Carolina's third-oldest city, with a charming waterfront, historic homes, and great seafood. Worth an afternoon. ~ 45 min.

🏖️ Pawleys Island

An unspoiled barrier island beach with a laid-back old-South character. Known for hammocks, beach cottages, and very few crowds. ~ 25 min.

🏛️ Conway Historic Downtown

A beautiful small-town waterfront on the Waccamaw River — antique shops, local restaurants, and historic homes under massive live oaks. ~ 30 min.

🎵 House of Blues Myrtle Beach

Live music venue at Barefoot Landing hosting national and regional acts — check the schedule before you arrive for a great night out.

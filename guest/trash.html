Bins are rolled out and returned on your behalf — you just need to make sure your trash is in the bins before collection day.

📍 Bin Location

Bins are normally located on the right side of the property. However, parking and other factors may require them to be moved — check around the property if you don't see them.

Please make sure bins are not blocked so they can be retrieved and returned on collection days.

♻️ What Goes Where

🟢 Green bin — Trash: General household waste, food scraps, non-recyclables

🔵 Blue bin — Recycling: Plastic bottles, cans, cardboard, glass — please rinse before recycling

No hazardous materials, chemicals, or large items in either bin.

🏖️ Beach & Pool Area

Please pick up any trash around the pool and outdoor areas throughout your stay. Extra trash bags are located under the kitchen sink.

🧹 Before Checkout

Remove all trash from inside the property and place in the outdoor bins before you leave. If bins are full, bag extra trash and leave neatly beside the bin.

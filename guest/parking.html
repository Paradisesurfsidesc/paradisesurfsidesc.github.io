🚗 Parking

Parking details and local rules for your stay at Paradise.

🅿️ Property Parking

Parking is available in the driveway under and around the property. Please keep vehicles within the designated parking area and do not block neighboring driveways or the road.

Maximum vehicles: 7 — please confirm with Southern Coast Vacations if you have a larger group.

🏖️ Beach Parking

Street parking is available near the beach access points in Surfside Beach. Arrive early during peak season to secure a spot. Some areas may have time restrictions — look for posted signage.

📋 Surfside Beach Parking Info ↗

⚠️ Local Rules

• Do not park on grass or landscaped areas

• Do not block fire hydrants or mailboxes

• Overnight street parking may be restricted in some areas — check local signage

• Surfside Beach enforces parking regulations — violations may result in fines

❓ Questions?

Contact Southern Coast Vacations at 843-663-6063 option 2 if you have questions about parking for your group size.

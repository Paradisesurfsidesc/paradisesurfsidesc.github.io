✅ Checkout

Check-out is at 10:00 AM. Please complete the following before you leave. Tap each item to check it off.

  • Remove all trash from the property and place in the appropriate bins outside.
  • Clean and put away all dirty dishes or run the dishwasher.
  • Close and lock all exterior doors and windows.
  • Set thermostat to 75°F (summer) or 55°F (winter).
  • Put all towels and provided linens in the linen bag. Leave mattress pads, encasements, and comforters on the bed unless soiled — if soiled, place beside the linen bag.
  • Clean the grill if used.
  • Pick up any trash around the pool area.
  • Turn off all lights, fans, and televisions.
  • Log out of any personal streaming accounts on the Roku TVs.
  • Double-check for personal belongings — we are not responsible for lost or left behind items.
  • Lock all doors and return keys to the lockbox they were retrieved from.

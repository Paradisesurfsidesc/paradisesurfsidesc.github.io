🏊 Pool

Your private heated and cooled pool — enjoy it any time during your stay.

🌡️ Heat & Cool

The pool is privately heated and cooled. Temperature is pre-set for your comfort. If you have concerns about the pool temperature contact Southern Coast Vacations at 843-663-6063 option 2.

🕐 Hours

The pool is available for guest use at any time during your stay. Please be respectful of neighbors during early morning and late evening hours.

📋 Pool Rules

• No glass in or around the pool area

• Please shower before entering the pool

• Children must be supervised at all times

• No diving

• Please pick up any trash around the pool before checkout

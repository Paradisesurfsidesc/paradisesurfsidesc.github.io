🏊 Pool

Your private heated and cooled pool — enjoy it any time during your stay.

🌡️ Heat & Cool

The pool is equipped with a heat pump for heating and cooling. Temperature changes are not instant — please allow several hours for the pool to reach your desired temperature.

Important: Pool heating and cooling is an additional charge. The temperature is locked and controlled exclusively by Southern Coast Vacations. Guests may not adjust pool equipment or settings.

For temperature concerns contact Southern Coast at 843-663-6063 option 2.

⚠️ No Lifeguard on Duty

There is no lifeguard on duty at this pool. All swimming is at your own risk.

Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times. Never leave children unattended in or around the pool.

🕐 Hours

Open: 8:00 AM (approximate — please confirm with Southern Coast)

Close: 11:00 PM

Please be respectful of neighbors during evening hours. No pool use after 11:00 PM.

📋 Pool Rules

• No glass in or around the pool area

• Shower before entering the pool

• Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times

• Children who are not toilet trained must wear a swim diaper — regular diapers are not permitted in the pool

• No diving or jumping from pool edges

• No running on the pool deck

• No roughhousing or dunking

• Inflatables must be reasonable in size and appropriate for the pool — oversized floats are not permitted

• No pets in the pool

• No pool use after 11:00 PM

• Keep noise to a minimum during evening hours

• Pick up all trash around the pool area throughout your stay

• Do not tamper with or attempt to adjust any pool equipment. All pool equipment is managed exclusively by Southern Coast Vacations — contact them for any issues at 843-663-6063 option 2

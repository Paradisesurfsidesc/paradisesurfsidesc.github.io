🏊 Pool

Your private heated and cooled pool — enjoy it during your stay.

⚠️

No Lifeguard on Duty

All swimming is at your own risk. Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times. Never leave children unattended in or around the pool.

🕐 Hours

Open: 8:00 AM (approximate — please confirm with Southern Coast)

Close: 11:00 PM · No pool use after 11:00 PM

Please be respectful of neighbors during evening hours.

📋 Pool Rules

• No glass in or around the pool area

• Shower before entering the pool

• Children must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times

• Children who are not toilet trained must wear a swim diaper — regular diapers are not permitted in the pool

• No diving or jumping from pool edges

• No running on the pool deck

• No roughhousing or dunking

• Inflatables must be reasonable in size and appropriate for the pool — oversized floats are not permitted

• No pets in the pool

• No pool use after 11:00 PM

• Keep noise to a minimum during evening hours

• Pick up all trash around the pool area throughout your stay

• Do not tamper with or attempt to adjust any pool equipment. All pool equipment is managed exclusively by Southern Coast Vacations

🌡️ Heat & Cool

The pool is equipped with a heat pump. Temperature changes are not instant — please allow several hours for the pool to reach your desired temperature.

Important: Pool heating and cooling is an additional charge. The temperature is locked and controlled exclusively by Southern Coast Vacations. Guests may not adjust pool equipment or settings.

Welcome to Paradise! Please take a few minutes to walk through the property and complete this check-in inspection.

⚠️ Important — Report Issues & Damage Immediately

Please walk through the entire property upon arrival and report any pre-existing damage, cleanliness concerns, or missing items immediately using the form below.

Any damage discovered during or after your stay may result in damage charges being applied to the current guest. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Broken windows, doors, or screens
  • Damaged floors, walls, or furniture
  • Evidence of smoking, vaping, or e-cigarettes inside the property
  • Damage to pool equipment or pool area
  • Missing or damaged appliances, TVs, or remotes
  • Stains or damage to linens, towels, or bedding

Reporting pre-existing damage at check-in protects you and ensures you are not held responsible for something that was not caused by you.

Southern Coast Vacations is committed to resolving any issue in a reasonable and timely manner during your stay — but we can only help if we know about it. Do not wait until departure or leave concerns in a review.

✅ Walk-Through Checklist

Tap each item as you check it. If anything looks off, report it below.

  • All exterior doors and locks are working properly
  • All windows open, close, and lock correctly
  • All bedrooms checked — beds, linens, and furniture in good condition
  • All bathrooms clean and in working order
  • Kitchen appliances working — stove, microwave, dishwasher, coffee maker
  • All TVs and remotes working
  • WiFi connecting properly
  • AC and thermostats working
  • Pool area clean and in good condition
  • Grill clean and ready for use
  • Both balconies checked — furniture and railings in good condition
  • Outdoor shower working
  • No visible damage to walls, floors, or furniture

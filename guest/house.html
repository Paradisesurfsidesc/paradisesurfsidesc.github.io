🏠 The House

Everything you need to know about TVs, AC, laundry, kitchen, and the grill.

📺 TVs & Streaming

All TVs are Roku TVs. Use the Roku remote to navigate.

Local cable is available via the Spectrum TV app — open it from the Roku home screen. No login required, it works automatically on the property network.

You can also log into your own streaming apps (Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, etc.) from the Roku home screen. Please remember to log out before you leave.

❄️ AC

All thermostats are Ecobee smart thermostats. Please keep them at a reasonable setting, to avoid freeze ups.

Important: The Ecobee system will automatically disable the AC if doors or windows are left open. Please keep all exterior doors and windows closed while the AC is running.

Departure: Set the AC to 75°F in summer, and the heat to 55°F in winter before you leave.

🧺 Washer & Dryer

The washer and dryer are located in the upstairs laundry room.

The washer is front-load. The dryer is top-load — the lint trap is located inside the dryer drum, please clean it before each use.

Courtesy laundry detergent is provided but is not guaranteed to be available. Please do not overload the machines.

🍳 Kitchen

The kitchen is equipped at the start of the season with cookware, utensils, plates, glasses, and basic appliances including a coffee maker, microwave, and dishwasher.

Note: Items can disappear over the course of the season. If something is missing that you need, please let us know via a maintenance request.

Please clean and put away all dishes before checkout.

🔥 Grill

A gas grill and propane are provided as a courtesy. Please clean and maintain the grill throughout your stay — not just at checkout.

Important: Propane tanks are to be exchanged by Southern Coast Vacations only. Do not attempt to swap or refill the tank yourself. If propane runs low contact Southern Coast at 843-663-6063 option 2.

Turn off the gas completely after every use.

🏖️ Beach Gear

Beach gear is not provided as part of your stay. Any chairs, umbrellas, or equipment found at the property were left by prior guests and are available on a communal, as-is basis.

Beach gear rentals are available locally. Rental options coming soon.

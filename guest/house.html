🏠 The House

Everything you need to know about TVs, AC, laundry, kitchen, and the grill.

📺 TVs & Streaming

All TVs are Roku TVs. Use the Roku remote to navigate.

Local cable is available via the Spectrum TV app — open it from the Roku home screen. No login required, it works automatically on the property network.

You can also log into your own streaming apps (Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, etc.) from the Roku home screen. Please remember to log out before you leave.

❄️ AC & Thermostat

Please keep the thermostat at a reasonable setting.

Departure: Set to 75°F in summer, 55°F in winter before you leave.

Do not leave doors or windows open while AC is running.

🧺 Washer & Dryer

The washer and dryer are located in the upstairs laundry room. Detergent pods are provided under the sink. Please do not overload the machines.

🍳 Kitchen

The kitchen is fully equipped with cookware, utensils, plates, glasses, and basic appliances. A coffee maker, microwave, and dishwasher are all available.

Please clean and put away all dishes before checkout.

🔥 Grill

A gas grill is available for guest use. Please clean the grill after each use and turn off the gas completely when done.

If the propane runs low during your stay contact Southern Coast Vacations at 843-663-6063 option 2.

🏖️ Beach Gear

Beach chairs, umbrellas, and boogie boards are available for guest use. Please rinse off sand before returning items and store them back in the designated area.
